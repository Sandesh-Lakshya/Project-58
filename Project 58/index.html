My Favourite Hobby

Reading Books

About

My hobby is reading books. I feel it very interesting whenever I get knowledge about new things. I like story books, encyclopedias and adventurous books. I mostly read these books when I am feeling bored or tired. After coming back from school, I love reading story books to refresh myself. Before going to bed, I always prefer reading some bedtime stories. It makes me happy and I really have a good sleep. Since childhood, I am great fond of books and it has become my passion now. No doubt, books are our best companion for life!
